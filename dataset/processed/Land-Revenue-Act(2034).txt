An Act Made to Provide for Land Revenue
Preamble : Whereas, it is expedient to amend and consolidate the provisions
relating to collection and recovery of the land rev enue and to make some

as the "Land Revenue Act, 2034 (1978)."

specified by Government of Nepal, by a Notification published in the Nepal
Gazette.
